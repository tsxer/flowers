Allergy Information

Plant Name Allergen Source Symptoms Recommendations
Ragweed Pollen Runny nose, itchy eyes, sneezing Avoid direct contact. Keep away during the blooming season.
Ficus Plant Sap Skin irritation, potential contact dermatitis Wear gloves when handling. Avoid sap contact with skin.
Lavender Strong Fragrance Headaches, throat irritation Avoid placing in bedrooms or near sensitive individuals.
Monstera Plant Sap Mucous membrane irritation, skin burning Keep away from children and pets.
Jasmine Fragrance Dizziness, respiratory irritation Use in well-ventilated areas.
